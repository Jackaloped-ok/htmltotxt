Translation Notes From Verthandi:

Nasca
Also "Nazca." This ancient South American civilization etched mysterious geometric patterns into the earth. The pictures formed by the Nasca Lines are only comprehensible when viewed from hundreds of miles above the Earth. Their purpose and the method of their construction are still unknown.
