Translation Notes From Verthandi:

Salamander - A flaming lizard, often found as a heraldic supporter, one of two animals that
flanks a crest.
Urchin - The heraldic name of the hedgehog, often found as a heraldic supporter, one of two animals that flanks a crest.

Charge - The decorative elements on a heraldic crest.
Field - The background color of a heraldic crest.

Or, Argent, Purpure, Vert, Gules, Azure, Sable
The heraldic names of the colors most commonly used on heraldic crests.
Or........Gold or Yellow.
Argent....Silver or white.
Purpure...Purple.
Vert......Green.
Gules.....Red.
Azure.....Blue.
Sable.....Black.

Ispahan - Also known as "Esfahan," a city known for beautiful architecture. The capital of Esfahan province in Iran. Also, a type of wild Iranian rose.
Caserta - Italian province.
Linderhof - Bavarian palace.
Cheval - Ferdinand Cheval, a surrealist architect who built Le Palais idéal (The Ideal Palace), a monument in Hauterives in the Rhône Valley.
Bomarzo - "The stone monsters of Bomarzo have been described as a uniquely grotesque assemblage of mythical, literary and heraldic sources that have, for generations, baffled and enthralled visitors."
Flora - Roman goddess of flowers.
Mathias - The apostle Matthew.
Versailles - The French city where Louis XIV built a magnificent palace.
Sahmek - The fifteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
Isola Bella - Italian island (lit. "Beautiful Island.")
Amphiteatro - It. "amphitheater."
Neuschwanstein - Bavarian palace
Santa Maria della Concezione - Italian cathedral.
