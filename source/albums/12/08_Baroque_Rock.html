Translation Notes From Hiraku:

Angels of Bernini
This is referring to Ponte Sant’Angelo, where it was believed that an angel appeared before people to herald the good news of the end of the plague. There are statues built at the bridge.

Satyr of Puget
I looked around and I can only find something about “Puget’s satyr” in some obscure source, namely the book titled “Rodin: Sculpture & Drawings” By Catherine Lampert, Arts Council of Great Britain.
