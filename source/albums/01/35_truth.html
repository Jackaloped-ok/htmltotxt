truth

Music: Rika Arai (新井理生)
Words: Shoko Fujibayashi (藤林聖子)
Arranged: Akihiko Hirama (平間あきひこ)
Vocal: Luka Yumi (裕未瑠華)
Track 35 of The Eve of the Absolute Evolution Revolution and track 28 of Advent of Nirvanic Beauty ~Androgynous Me~ are the full length version of this song.
Track 27 of Virtual Star Embryology is the TV version of this song.
Because there's only the bolded line's difference between these versions, the full length and TV versions are side by side.
